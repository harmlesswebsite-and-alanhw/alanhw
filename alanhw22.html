HOW TO MAKE MAC&CHEESE

  1. Get Macaroni Pasta and Boil it.
  2. Make cheese sauce by heating up cheese, or you can combine butter and flour
  3. Stir the Cheese sauce with the Macaroni Pasta
  4. Put Half of the macaroni and cheese onto a plate
  5. Add more shredded cheese
  6. Then Bake the mixture
  7. Lastly, you will have a tasty serving of mac and cheese
